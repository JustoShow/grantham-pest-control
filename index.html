About Us

Grantham Pest Control is a licensed local Houston professional pest control company that guarantees to get rid of your insect problems, while our protection services make sure your home or workplace is always protected.

Our Mission

Grantham Pest Control is committed to providing high quality pest control services by combining tried and tested methods with new and innovative products to effectively rid your home or workplace of unsightly and damaging pests. Our trained and licensed staff provide courteous and prompt service as we assess the infestation and design a solution to suit your needs. Our products are applied with methods that are environmentally safe to your staff, workplace, home, family and pets. We have been in business for over 17 years and are committed to providing you with rock-solid service and information that will help you maintain a pest free environment. Are bugs bugging you?
